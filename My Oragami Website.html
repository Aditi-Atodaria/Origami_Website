Interesting Facts About Panda

  • Pandas go from pink to white and black (or brown)
  • An adult can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day!
  • Pandas have carnivorous teeth, but they eat bamboo and fruit.
  • Prehistoric pandas lived up to 2 million years ago.

Interesting Facts About Cicada

  • Most have red-orange eyes. But occasionally cicadas have blue, white or grayish eyes.
  • Cicadas have one of the longest insect lifespans
  • They can buzz louder than a lawnmower
  • There are more than 3,000 species of Cicada that exist

Interesting Facts About Camel

  • There are two types of camels: One humped or “dromedary” camels and two humped Bactrian camels.
  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  • Camels can completely shut their nostrils during sandstorms.
  • Their humps let them store up to 80 pounds of fat which they can live off for weeks and even months!
